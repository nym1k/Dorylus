About The Band

Dorylus are a hard-hitting 4 piece metal band from Hertfordshire that plays Melodic Thrash/Death Metal. Since our inception in January 2015 we’ve released our EP ‘The Rapture’ in December of the same year, and have obtained a strong following in all areas of the country we’ve reached so far.

On top of this we played in the Grand Final of London Metal 2 The Masses 2016, and in 2017 completed our first two tours, one being alongside Swedish Death Metal fiends Creeping Flesh as well as sharing the stage with acts such as Sumer, Krysthla and King Leviathan.

With influences ranging from classic bands like Iron Maiden and Slayer to modern acts such as Lamb of God and Amon Amarth, we promise to deliver the perfect blend of face melting, bone crunching heavy metal and ensure that if you see us you won’t regret it!
